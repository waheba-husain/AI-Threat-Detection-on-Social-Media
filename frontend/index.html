About
Built for the
Safety Challenge

SentinelAI is a final year engineering project built to address the growing crisis of harmful content on social media — from hate speech to extremist recruitment to suicide risk signals.

We fine-tuned a RoBERTa transformer model on a balanced dataset of 4 threat categories, building a full-stack platform for detection, reporting, and response.
